Welcome to Pendleton Solutions

Pendleton Solutions, LLC is a minority small woman-owned business. We are dedicated to security management, cybersecurity, and information technology. We offer large scale business solutions with small business intimacy and customer focused attitudes. Founded in 2014, by a senior defense security expert, we have provided holistic national security support to the federal government and commercial entities across different continents.

"Secure solutions guaranteed" is our motto. We take a tailored approach to helping our clients build and maintain their security programs. Our employees are leaders in the security industry, and is focused on putting our customer’s mission first. We are training and empowering the next generation of security professionals to protect the interest of national security.

- Tenia Mackall

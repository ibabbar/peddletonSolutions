Pendleton Solutions, LLC is a SBA women-owned small business (WOSB) and economically disadvantaged women-owned small business (EDWOSB). The SBA WOSB program authorizes Federal agencies and organizations’ Contracting Officers to restrict competition by setting aside certain procurement requirements solely for WOSBs or EDWOSBs. The Federal government must award 5% of its prime and subcontract dollars to WOSBs/EDWOSBs.

Benefits of Procuring through the WOSB/EDWOSB Program

Limited Competition- Agencies benefit from limited competition procurement for service contracts without restrictions to the size of the contract. The competitive WOSB or EDWOSB procurement requires only two (2) WOSB or EDWOSB firms that are capable of satisfying the agency’s requirements.

Small Business Credits- Agencies can receive small business participation credits for participating in the WOSB/EDWOSB program.
